# Jurisdiction notes: Australia vs Aotearoa New Zealand (life insurance)

This file captures *working notes* for configuring comparators and interpreting policy levers.
It is not legal advice. Validate all definitions and effective dates.

## Australia (life insurance)
- Industry moratorium introduced July 2019 with illustrative limits:
  - $500,000 death/TPD; $200,000 trauma; $4,000/month income protection.
- A statutory prohibition has been proposed via the Treasury Laws Amendment (Genetic Testing Protections in Life Insurance and Other Measures) Bill 2025 (introduced 26 Nov 2025). The parliamentary digest describes a ban on using protected genetic information and associated changes to disclosure duties and the Disability Discrimination Act exemption.

## Aotearoa New Zealand (life insurance)
- No equivalent moratorium reported in Australasian clinical guidance; insurers can ask applicants to disclose genetic testing outcomes and use these in underwriting.
- This repo includes illustrative moratorium/ban comparators to support a quantitative appraisal of reform options.
