## Email Template

**Subject:** Invitation to Review: Genetic Discrimination Policy Economic Evaluation Model

**Dear [Dr/Prof Name],**

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to invite you to serve as an expert reviewer for a research project developing an economic evaluation framework for genetic discrimination policies in Australia and New Zealand.

### About the Research

This project develops a modular Bayesian decision analysis model to quantify the economic and welfare impacts of policies restricting genetic discrimination in life insurance. The model covers:

- **Behavioural responses** (testing uptake, deterrence)
- **Insurance equilibrium** (adverse selection, premium impacts)
- **Clinical outcomes** (QALYs, health system costs)
- **Proxy substitution** (family history use)
- **Market structure** (pass-through, competition)
- **Data quality externalities** (research participation)

### Review Request

I am seeking your expertise to review the **model structure and assumptions** for face validity. Specifically:

1. **Model structure** — Does the causal framework make sense?
2. **Key assumptions** — Are they reasonable and well-justified?
3. **Parameter choices** — Do prior distributions align with evidence?
4. **Policy relevance** — Will outputs be useful for decision makers?

### Review Package

The review package includes:
- Model structure documentation (~25 pages)
- Evidence registers (Australia + New Zealand)
- Calibration configurations
- Structured questionnaire

**Time commitment:** ~2-3 hours

**Timeline:** Feedback requested by [date, 2 weeks from invitation]

### Why You?

Your expertise in [health economics / insurance markets / genetic policy] makes you ideally suited to evaluate this model. Your insights will help ensure the research is rigorous and policy-relevant.

### Next Steps

If you are willing to review, please let me know and I will send the review package.

Thank you for considering this request.
